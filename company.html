about

we help brands & products achieve goals

We do
Tubik is a group of people united by the desire to work with various companies and increase their capital with the help of websites, mobile applications, graphic design, and creative projects. Our goal is to create a substantial result for the client.
We form small teams for the necessary tasks, which can consist of UI/UX designers, graphic designers, artists, motion designers, and other specialists. Since 2013, we have constantly been changing in search of ourselves and organically came to the format of an agency that can create digital products and design systems of any level.
